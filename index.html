Spin & Spill Campaign

A creative campaign concept for a Boston-based boutique spin studio. Spin & Spill: Playlist Confessional is a July ride series built on community-sourced playlists, storytelling, and post-ride connection. This multi-touch campaign was designed to drive retention, deepen emotional engagement, and build a routine anchored in ritual and catharsis.

The concept is simple but powerful: each Saturday in July, riders submit a song that connects to a weekly theme (exes, summer memories, empowerment, etc.), and the playlist for that week's class is built around their submissions. It's a ride that's crowdsourced and cathartic. Themes are announced weekly via Instagram and in-studio materials, and riders submit songs using a QR code that leads to a living Google Form or info page. Submissions can include stories, allowing instructors to create personal, engaging moments during class (like lighthearted or emotional shoutouts). If a song isn't spin-friendly, instructors can find a remix so no story is left behind.

The goal of the series is to create loyalty, build routine, and help riders feel seen through music — whether they're healing, spiraling, laughing, or just vibing. It's boutique fitness with real emotional texture, wrapped in humor and community.

The campaign expands into three branded experiences:

  • Spin & Spill Storytime – Instructor-led anecdotes and member-submitted shoutouts shared mid-ride and on social via Reels
  • Spin & Spill Playlists – Themed weekly submissions turned into remixable ride soundtracks and Spotify shares
  • Spin & Spill Saturdays – Espresso martinis, mimosas, and post-ride connection — think mini-brunch energy with confession prompts and playlist recaps

The campaign is supported by a "This & That" Instagram carousel series emphasizing wellness duality and humor, plus custom activation ideas such as:

  • Boston Calling festival hydration lounge + confessional tent
  • Campus pop-ups and "Date Drop" gamified class pricing
  • Neighborhood takeover challenge and referral incentives

APSIA Ad: Thrive Amid Uncertainty

One of my most innovative approaches to advertising, developed through comprehensive Reddit research. I built a custom Python web scraper to analyze countless discussions about graduate programs in international relations, identifying key concerns in the current political climate. The data revealed anxieties about career prospects, program ROI, and how changing global politics affects the value of IR degrees.

This data-driven approach led to our powerful "Thrive amid uncertainty, lead with impact" tagline that directly addresses these concerns. The final creative was selected through extensive A/B testing on LinkedIn to determine the highest-performing imagery and messaging combination.
